Healing Your Tattoo

Aftercare Guide

Proper aftercare ensures your tattoo heals beautifully and lasts a lifetime.

01

Keep It Covered

Leave the initial wrap on for 2–4 hours (or as instructed). Your tattoo is an open wound — protect it.

02

Gentle Washing

Wash with lukewarm water and fragrance-free soap 2–3 times daily. Pat dry — never rub.

03

Moisturize

Apply a thin layer of unscented lotion (Lubriderm, Aquaphor) 2–3x daily. Don't over-apply.

04

Avoid Soaking

No pools, hot tubs, or long baths for 2–3 weeks. Showers are fine — just keep them short.

05

No Picking or Scratching

Peeling is normal. Let it happen naturally. Picking can pull ink and damage your design.

06

Sun Protection

Stay out of direct sun for 3 weeks. Once healed, always apply SPF 50+ to protect your ink.
